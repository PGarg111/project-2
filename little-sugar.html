Little Sugar Creek Greenway

Little Sugar Creek Greenway is a multi-mile paved trail corridor running north to south through Charlotte, extending from North Brevard Street near Uptown, all the to the South Carolina state line—spanning roughly 16 to 19 miles depending on completed segments. The greenway connects urban and natural highlights like Cordelia Park, Freedom Park, Midtown Park, Nature Museum, Central Pedimont Community College, and cultural districts.
