STK Steakhouse

STK Steakhouse offers a modern, upscale twist on the classic steakhouse experience. Located in the heart of Charlotte, STK combines a chic, contemporary setting with a menu featuring prime cuts of steak, fresh seafood, and indulgent sides. Known for its vibrant ambiance and exceptional service, STK is the perfect destination for a night out, whether you're celebrating a special occasion or enjoying a delicious meal with friends. With signature cocktails, an extensive wine list, and a high-energy atmosphere, STK elevates the traditional steakhouse experience to something extraordinary.
