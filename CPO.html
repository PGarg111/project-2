Charlotte Premium Outlets

Charlotte Premium Outlets is a outdoor outlet mall located in the Steele Creek area of southwest Charlotte. It opened in 2014 and is co-owned by Simon Property Group and Tanger Outlet Centers. There are many designers and name brands such as:

  • Michael Kors
  • Nike Factory Store
  • Coach Outlet
  • Saks OFF 5th
  • Ann Taylor Factory
  • Brooks Brothers
  • Kate Spade
  • Vera Bradley
  • Polo Ralph Lauren
The outlet mall offers daily discouts ranging from 25% to 65% off. The open-air layout features multiple buildings, elegant walkways, fountains, and covered areas for comfort, creating a relaxed and pleasantly walkable shopping environment. Visitors can enjoy free Wi-Fi, ATMs, family restrooms, nursing lounges, stroller and wheelchair rentals, ride-share zones, and ample parking.
