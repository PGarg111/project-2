Dogwood Southern Table & Bar

Dogwood Southern Table & Bar brings the comforting flavors of the South to the heart of Charlotte. With a menu that highlights fresh, locally sourced ingredients, Dogwood offers a modern twist on Southern classics like shrimp and grits, fried chicken, and house-made biscuits. Pair your meal with craft cocktails or an extensive selection of local beers in a cozy, rustic setting. Whether you're gathering for brunch, dinner, or a special night out, Dogwood provides a warm and inviting atmosphere where Southern hospitality shines.
