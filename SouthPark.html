SouthPark Mall

SouthPark Mall, located 5-6 miles south of Uptown Charlotte, is the largest mall in the Carolinas, the 10th largest on the East Coast, and the 28th larges in the US. The mall opened in February 1970 and was developed by the Belk and the Ivey families, later became a part of the Simon Property Group. There are over 170-180 stores, which include high-end anchors like:

  • Belk
  • Dillard's
  • Dick's Sporting Goods
  • Macy's
  • Neiman Marcus
  • Nordstrom
The mall also offers luxury stores such as:
  • Gucci
  • Louis Vuitton
  • Saint Laurent
  • Burberry
  • Tiffany & Co.
  • Alexander McQueen
Accross the mall is the Village at Southpark, a five story mixed-use development featuring upscale retail (anchored by Crate & Barrel) and 150 luxury residential units. The Symphony Park is an outdoor amphitheater and green space-hosts over 80 events anually, including the popular Charlotte Symphony "Summer Pops" concerts. There’s also a planned $21 million revitalization of the park with new restaurants, retail spaces, enhanced bandshell, and improved pedestrian access, expected to kick off in 2026 and finish by 2027.
