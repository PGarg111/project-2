The Market at 7th Street

The Market at 7th Street is a dynamic food hall and gathering space in the heart of Charlotte’s historic 7th Street. Offering a curated selection of local food vendors, craft drinks, and artisanal goods, The Market provides a vibrant, community-focused atmosphere. Whether you're in the mood for a quick bite, a leisurely meal, or unique shopping finds, it’s the perfect spot to connect, explore, and enjoy the flavors of the city.
