Concord Mills

Concord Mills is North Carolina's largest indoor outlet and value retail shopping destination, with over 200 stores under one roof. Its located about 10-12 miles north of Uptown Charlotte. Its a single-story, oval-shaped building. The main corridor forms a continuous loop lined with shops on both sides. The mall offers a blend on value outlet stores which include:

  • Coach
  • Michael Kors Outlet
  • Polo Ralph Lauren Factory Store
  • Kate Space
  • Bass Pro Shops Outdoor World
  • Adidas
Concord Mills has quite a few entertainment options. It features a 24-screen AMC/IMAX theater, the SEA LIFE Charlotte-Concord Aquarium, The Escape Game, Dave & Buster's, and The Speedpark.
