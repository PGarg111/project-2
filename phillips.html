Phillips Place

Phillips Place is an upscale mixed-use center since its opening in 1997. It blends boutique retail, refined dining, residential, and hospitality in a walkable, main-street layout. Offers over 25 specialty shops and premier restaurants, spanning fashion, home goods, accessories, bridal, and more. Currently, Phillips Place is undergoing a $50 million reinvention led by Lincoln Harris. It's building upon its village aesthetic with improved public spaces and high-end tenants. The plans also include an impactful 10-story office tower and expansion of commercial space-bringing new verticality to SouthPark's skyline.
