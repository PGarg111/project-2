Overview

Charlotte, known as the Queen City, is the largest city in North Carolina and a major U.S. financial hub. Famous for its modern skyline, Charlotte is home to the NASCAR Hall of Fame, the U.S. National Whitewater Center, and a vibrant arts scene.
