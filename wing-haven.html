Wing Haven

Wing Haven is a garden and bird sanctuary located about 3 miles from Uptown Charlotte and was founded by Elizabeth and Edwin Clarkson in 1927. It is Charlotte's only local historic landmark garden and bird sanctuary. Over the decades, more than 150 bird species have been spotted here. The garden contains:

  • Historic Garden & Bird Refuge
  • Elizabeth Lawrence House & Garden
  • SEED Wildlife & Children's Garden
